- 3 = Acceptable: major requirements are present, but there are noticeable omissions or weak coverage.
- 4 = Good: nearly all material requirements are covered, with only minor omissions.
- 5 = Excellent: all material ground-truth requirements are clearly and adequately covered.

Evaluation Steps:
1. Read the ground truth requirements and treat them as the checklist of required content.
2. Read the generated SRS and judge whether each material ground-truth requirement is clearly covered.
3. Ignore writing style and ignore extra unsupported details for this metric; focus only on missing versus covered required content.
4. Assign a single score from 1 to 5.
